Rules of the game

  • A caller is chosen to draw and announce numbers.
  • Players buy tickets with 15 random numbers on them.
  • The caller draws numbers from 1-90 randomly and announces them.
  • Players cross off announced numbers on their tickets.
  • Players can win small amount by claiming Fast Five, First, Second and Third Lines
  • The first player to cross off all numbers in a winning pattern shouts “Housie”. There might be more than 1 Housie based on (number of player/amount collected at the start of game).
  • The caller verifies the winning ticket.
  • A player can only claim a win before the next number is announced.
  • If multiple players win simultaneously, the prize is shared.
  • False claims are considered fouls.
  • The game ends when all numbers are drawn or all prizes are won.
